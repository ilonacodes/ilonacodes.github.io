1. Overview

There are many ways of discovering Berlin: with a travel guide or an informative website or on the spot with a practical app. The Berlin Wall Quiz is an interactive app which helps users to get more information or learn the history and interesting facts about the Berlin Wall. Photos, audio, and video clips, texts are presented in historical chronology. At the end of the every chronological time slot, there is an opportunity to start a quiz and see results after the completion.

3. Research & Personas

I have described user personas based on a broad target market that would use the Berlin Wall Quiz app. Visual information categorized chronologically because users want to get the information about a specific time frame.

What users would expect from this kind of app:

  • The platform that easy to use on the go. Definitely, it would be a mobile app
  • Supporting landscape screen to make it easy to use audio/video controls; make videos bigger by increasing their size to full-screen
  • Testing the gained knowledge using quiz feature
  • Implementing the way to review the app and give it ratings
  • Opportunity to share interesting audio records, video clips, texts with other people via social media

What business expects from this app:

  • Increased user base growth through partially locked content that is unlocked by sharing the parts of the application with friends

4. Wireframes & User Flows



Welcome Screen

With app specific images. Slide through the views to start using the app.


Timeline Overview

The story of the city and Berlin Wall in a unique circular timeline, each point on the navigation represents a milestone in the story.


Timeline Screen

Once a timeline story has been selected, the page shows the full story of the chosen milestone. This page can contain multiple elements like text, images, video, audio, etc.


Timeline Continued

Some content is locked, tapping on this will launch a window showing the user how to unlock the feature. Throughout the app, a share button will activate the native share dialog, where users can share parts of the app. This will provide unlocking functionality.


Landscape Screen

Ensure the app supports landscape mode for screens that make sense, for example: movie player screen, audio player, timeline overview.


Main Navigation

It will sit in the top right corner (top left when open) and can be accessed from all areas of the app.


Audio Player

This page acts as a portal to all audio files within the timeline page. All audio files are listed for direct play.


Movie Player

This page acts as a portal to all movies within the timeline page. All videos are available for immediate playing.


Quiz

Users can take a quiz any time when they want.


Results

After completing the quiz, users are able to see their results.


Reviews

Users can read the latest review posts, and also create their own. Adding a review unlocks a new feature.
